Frequently Asked Questions

What is a refurbished device?
Our refurbished devices are second-hand and have received a makeover from the inside out. This involves rigorous testing carried out by trained professionals to ensure that they're working flawlessly when they arrive with you. All our devices are supported by their manufacturers, meaning they'll keep receiving regular software and security updates. They're also refurbished using genuine manufacturer parts, and are not only up to 70% cheaper than buying new but are also much better for the environment! By buying refurbished, you can save up to 62kg of CO2 vs buying a new phone, depending on the model. That's the equivalent of a flight from London to Amsterdam!

How devices are tested?
All stock has been processed through our industry-leading 75 point inspection process. Through this process, we conduct a full check to make sure your device is fully functional, testing things including the battery capacity, LCD/screen, Wi-Fi, call connectivity, home/power button, volume button.

What can you expect from the battery?
We test all our batteries and ensure they have at least 80% of their initial charging capacity to ensure the efficiency of your phone, no matter the grade. Most major manufacturers consider any device with a battery capacity of 80% or above to be in optimal condition. This should be more than enough to keep you going throughout the day with normal use, meaning you can carry on chatting, gaming, or watching videos to your heart's content.

When will my order be dispatched?
All orders received before 1 pm are shipped the same day (excluding bank holidays) - this means you won't have to wait long for your order!

What level of guarantee do Loop offer?
We offer a 30-day hassle-free money back guarantee - we'll even accept a return if you change your mind with your purchase.
